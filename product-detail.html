Kaju Katli is one of India's most loved sweets — a diamond-shaped confection made from finely ground cashews cooked with sugar syrup and a touch of ghee, finished with edible silver leaf (chandi vark). Each piece is handcrafted in small batches to preserve the delicate, melt-in-the-mouth texture our customers love.

Perfect for gifting during festivals, weddings, or simply as a treat for your loved ones.

  • Premium whole cashews (Kaju)
  • Refined sugar
  • Pure ghee
  • Edible silver leaf (Chandi Vark)
  • Cardamom (Elaichi) — trace

Contains: Tree nuts (Cashew). Made in a facility that also processes milk and other nuts.
